# ARCHETYPE LIBRARY (spec)

Layer: ACTIVE SPECS · Status: ACTIVE · Promoted from research/SPINE.md
§11–13 on 2026-06-12. Extends docs/COPY_SYSTEM.md §4 (the 12 base
archetype classes live there); this spec governs *selection and naming*.

## Law

**Archetypes are photo roles, not personality claims.**
Good: "This frame routes toward The Threshold." / "The styling reads as
controlled disruption."
Bad: "You are a rebel." / "You are seductive." / "You are an alpha."

## Selection formula

```
route + dominant stat pair + strongest receipt + contradiction code
```

The archetype must be defensible from receipts. No receipt → no claim.

## Naming formulas

```
The [Modifier] [Role]        The [Scene Token] [Role]
[Material] [Role]            [Trait] Witness
[Place/Scene] Courier        Saint of [Artifact Trait]
Keeper of [Visible Signal]
```

Safe example titles: The Encounter · The Dispatch · The Threshold ·
The Horizon · The Archive · The Apparition · The Field Warden ·
The Static Saint · The Signal Baron · The Soft Menace · The Ice Witness ·
The Late Courier.

Mythology is flavor, not a god list. Prefer Blue Room-native titles over
literal Zeus/Thor/Hades; avoid religious/cultural appropriation unless
deliberately curated. AI never invents titles without evidence backing.

## Banned wording (in archetypes, titles, and readings)

attractiveness · hotness · alpha/beta · biology · race · gender · age ·
health · intelligence · morality · sexuality · personality diagnosis.
(Full banned lexicon: COPY_SYSTEM.md §3.)

## Tone

Archival, dry, stylish, restrained, slightly funny. Never motivational,
never clinical, never horny, never moralizing.

> The room found a quiet frame with unusually high distance control.
> The jacket does most of the speaking. The background agrees.
