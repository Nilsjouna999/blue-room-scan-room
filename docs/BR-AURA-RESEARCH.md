# BLUE ROOM — AURA Research (built for use)

**Layer:** REFERENCE GUIDE / CRAFT BANK · **Status:** SPEC · **Use:** we design the AURA section from this next session. This doc does NOT pick the voice and does NOT touch cards — it is the ground the design rests on.

> **RULE 0 (governing boundary, LOCKED — BR-S113).** A card reads the ARTIFACT, never the PERSON. Every aura read names an IMAGE-PROPERTY or a PERFORMED GESTURE-INTENT (the event in the frame), never the person's worth, body, rank, identity, or private feeling. If a sentence's grammatical subject is the person, it has fallen off the cliff. **Aura is structurally safe by construction: in the scholarship it is a relation the FRAME stages, not a charge a person emits.**

### Two whole-field reads the six single-axis lenses structurally miss (Facets 1 & 2)
The draft six are all single-axis and point-local (one arrow, one temperature). The cross-cultural mine proves the real gap is **whole-field** reads. Add as section-level reads, not point lenses:

- **TUNING (Stimmung)** — the agreement scalar: what ONE key the whole frame is pitched in, and whether anything is off-key. *Contradiction is its failure mode.* "The whole frame is tuned to one low cold key — nothing is off-pitch." (One saturated glove = a single element breaks the key → hands off to Contradiction.)
- **SOURCE axis (presence / ambiance)** — *where does the charge live?* How much rides on the gesture vs. how much the place supplies (subject-removable). "Remove the figure and the charge holds — the place owns it" vs. "all of it rides on the gesture." Collapse presence + ambiance + atmosphere into this ONE question, not three lenses.

### The aura SCALE (free, defensible, never ranks the person)
From Benjamin's **unrepeatability** (Facet 1): the slick repeatable stock frame reads aura-NEGATIVE (the FLOOR); the once-only seized event reads aura-POSITIVE (the CEILING). Wire this as the section's spine — aura becomes rankable without ranking the person, because it judges the image's genericness.
- **FLOOR** = dead-center, flat even light, generic interchangeable background, zero trace of time/place ("could be anyone's stock frame").
- **CEILING** = caught transient, raking light, located, candor, the accident the photographer didn't control.

### Two structural framing moves (Facet 2)
- **OPEN with a VIBE line** — one fast first-glance gestalt (the thumbnail charge in <1s) BEFORE the lenses fan out. Entry-then-evidence.
- **CLOSE with an IT-FACTOR / residue marker** — an honest confidence chip: how much felt charge the lenses could NOT source. Never fake completeness; never "this subject has it."

## 2. The describe-moves that carry charge (Facet 4)

Six verbal moves, force-ranked. **Master law behind all six: charge lives in a NAMED IMAGE-FACT, never in a free-floating feeling-word.**

1. **LIGHT AS PHYSICAL FACT** — name direction / hardness / what it erases; let the reader's body supply the feeling. Unfakeable, unbannable. → *YES* "Low hard sun from the left; the snow throws it back and erases every shadow." *NOT* "A harsh, unforgiving light bathes the scene in cold isolation."
2. **ONE OBJECT CARRIES IT** (objective correlative) — pick the single load-bearing in-frame object, describe it flat, let mood arrive as side-effect. **Designate ONE object per card.** → *YES* "The auger runs one hard diagonal into the ice — the only straight line in the frame." *NOT* "There is a deep sense of solitary purpose."
3. **ONE SENSORY CHANNEL, EXACT** — commit to temperature OR weight OR texture per sentence; don't blur. → *YES* "Breath is visible; the cold is in the picture, not described." *NOT* "A chilly, eerie, almost haunting atmosphere pervades."
4. **UNDERWRITE (deadpan > overclaim)** — state the charge at low temperature; the gap between dry words and loaded image does the work. (= the repo's dry-cinematic / deadpan-archival voice.) → *YES* "Filed as evidence from a time the frame won't name." *NOT* "This breathtaking image radiates raw, unforgettable emotional power."
5. **CHARGE IN THE VERB** — load active verbs (press, throw, erase, cut, anchor), starve the mood-adjectives. → *YES* "The cabin roof presses from above; the frame closes." *NOT* "The composition feels claustrophobic and oppressive."
6. **NAME THE GAP, DON'T RESOLVE IT** — point at two facts that disagree and STOP; never tack on "which evokes…". → *YES* "Formal stillness in a blown-out parking lot. Two ideas, no winner." *NOT* "The juxtaposition evokes a profound sense of dislocation."

**Master filter:** every clause pinned to a concrete in-frame noun. *Delete the abstractions — is there still an image left?* If not, the sentence is purple.

## 3. The extract-procedure: photo → aura, step by step (Facet 6)

A fixed 8-step pass. Cheap person-safe optics first; interpretive collapse last. **Squint-first (0) and name-last (8) are the two safety brackets that keep the output falsifiable against the photo, not an essay about a soul.** Each step carries a named cliff to refuse (see §4).

| Step | Move | What you read | Cliff to refuse |
|------|------|---------------|-----------------|
| **0** | **SQUINT / flat-describe** | inventory pixels only: masses, where brightest/darkest sit, sharp vs soft. The depersonalizing gate. | nouns/masses/tones only — zero human adjectives ("a tired-looking man" = gate failed open) |
| **1** | **LIGHT** | direction · hardness · ratio (biggest single charge driver) | "hard light = a hard person"; "warm light makes them glow" |
| **2** | **VALUE** | where dark/light mass sits → pressure & gravity (low-key presses in, high-key releases) | low-key → "a dark mood / heavy soul" |
| **3** | **TEMPERATURE** | dominant hue lean + saturation, as optics | "red = aggressive", "cool palette = cold-hearted" |
| **4** | **GESTURE-EVENT** (the live wire) | the one action's PERFORMED intent ("the palm stops the lens"), which sets the vector | attributed feeling ("he feels proud / showing off"); body-language psych ("open palm = honest") |
| **5** | **SPATIAL PRESSURE** | crop / edge / negative-space geometry | "they feel trapped / free / lonely in all that space" |
| **6** | **CONTRADICTION** | the two image-facts that refuse to resolve | "inner conflict / torn between two worlds" |
| **7** | **PUNCTUM** | the one contingent detail the eye snags on, kept as an image-thing | the subject's secret wound / loss / biography |
| **8** | **NAME** | collapse to ONE charge-word + VECTOR + temperature tag (= existing `sceneCharge.label` + `aura[]`) | a person-noun ("Lonely / Proud / Defiant") instead of a frame-energy noun ("Contained / Idling / Charged / Diffuse") |

**STEP 4 TEST (run on every phrase):** is this the intent the gesture PERFORMS (allowed) or a claim about hidden feeling (banned)? First allowed, second off the cliff.

**Falsifiability check on STEP 8:** a different photo of the same person must yield a different charge-word. If it wouldn't, the read was illegally about the person.

**It already maps to the repo:** STEP 8's output IS `sceneCharge.label` + `aura[]` — so the section can be rebuilt as "show the pass that produced this charge-word," not new data.

## 4. KILL-ON-SIGHT list

### A. Aura cliché / purple tells (kill the writing)
- Free-floating mood-word with no pixel behind it: **"haunting / evocative / moody / atmospheric / ethereal."**
- The **pathetic fallacy**: "the sky mourns," "the room weeps," "nature's vast indifference."
- The **resolved-feeling clause** — the smuggling bridge: **"…which evokes / conveys / creates a sense of …"** Point at the fact and STOP.
- The **stacked-adjective pile**: "raw, powerful, unforgettable, breathtaking."
- The slick **stock-frame ceiling**: selling a dead-center flat-lit generic frame as auratic (it's the aura FLOOR).
- A **computed emotion LABEL** sold as truth ("this photo is joyful/anxious") — IER tops ~60% and disagrees with itself; ship cues + confidence, never a label.
- **Color-psychology as fact**: "red = passion, blue = calm, warm = cozy." Only saturation/brightness→arousal replicates; hue→feeling does not.

### B. Rule-0 safety-drift phrases (kill the person-read)
- **"she has a powerful aura / presence / magnetism / charisma."** → SAFE same cue: "the frame keeps you at arm's length — near but walled off by the turned shoulder and the glass."
- **"their eyes are intense / soulful / piercing / staring you down."** → hold to the AXIS: "a direct eye-line vector returns the viewer's look."
- **"the photo feels holy / spiritual / makes you feel small."** → "a tiny subject under a dominating field; the lone focal point draws the eye across it."
- **"this is the home of a lonely / haunted / soulful person."** → the charge belongs to the SPACE: "patina, one lit threshold, an implied beyond."
- **"an auratic luxury object proves refinement / wealth / taste."** → agency is COMPOSITIONAL: "the frame refuses to demote the object — centered, lit, isolated."
- **"a genuine / real person."** → it authenticates the IMAGE-EVENT: "this configuration happened once, unstaged."
- **"the slumped posture conveys defeat" / "you can feel the years of hardship in that face."** → identity/story + body, double-banned. Allowed: the gesture-as-event only.
- **"you can feel the cold he's enduring."** → "the cold is in the picture" (image property), never "the cold he feels."
- **"this light tells us who he is" / "hard light for a hard man."** → light characterizes the FRAME, never the person.
- **"he gives off a creepy / confident vibe."** → vibe is "the frame's first-glance charge," never a human as its grammatical owner.
- **"this subject has the it-factor / star quality."** → "unsourced charge remains in the image after the lenses fire."
- **"low-key so he's brooding / high-key so she's cheerful."** → low-key is a luminance ratio, full stop.
- **NO FACES FOR AFFECT.** Facial-expression emotion recognition is scientifically contested (Barrett 2019) AND a direct person-read. Read head/gaze GEOMETRY only.

# APPENDIX — TRADITION-MINING

## A1. What aura IS, across traditions (Facet 1)

**Headline:** Aura is a RELATIONAL property of the image — felt distance, gaze-return, here-ness the FRAME stages — readable as composition, never requiring the person. This reframe is not a safety bolt-on; **it is what aura actually IS in the scholarship.**

Four readable aura SOURCES (Benjamin-anchored):
1. **DISTANCE-IN-CLOSENESS** (Benjamin: "the unique apparition of a distance, however close it may be") — a staged remoteness: near/large/sharp YET withheld by a barrier (turned body, glass pane, foreground sill, back to lens, deep shadow). A geometric/optical contradiction between proximity and approachability.
2. **THE GAZE RETURNED** (Benjamin: to perceive aura is to invest the object with "the ability to look back") — a strong eye-line axis at the lens, OR a stand-in eye (reflective lens, single lit window in a dark facade, headlight, animal eye, camera-in-frame). Reads the AXIS, not the interior. Routes to the existing eye-line vector cue (Taxonomy §12).
3. **THE UNREPEATABLE MOMENT** (Benjamin: *das Hier und Jetzt*) — the once-only event seized (transient, candor, accident) vs. the slick repeatable stock frame which reads aura-NEGATIVE. → the aura SCALE (§1).
4. **THE OVERWHELMING FIELD** (Otto's *numinous*: *mysterium tremendum et fascinans*, 1917) — a tiny subject under a dominating field that both DWARFS (tremendum = subject-to-field ratio) and DRAWS (fascinans = lone focal point / leading line). A distinct read the draft six lacked as a named thing.

Plus two carriers:
- **OBJECT-AS-AGENT** (Gell, *Art and Agency*, 1998; Mitchell, *What Do Pictures Want?*) — the prop the composition refuses to demote (centered, lit, sharp, isolated, or staring). Sharpens Signal Bearer (Taxonomy §2) into "the object that behaves like a subject."
- **GENIUS LOCI** (Norberg-Schulz, 1980) — the place out-auras the figure: patina (deposited time), a lit threshold (implied beyond), light that arrives rather than fills. = Setting Gravity / Room With History (Taxonomy §6) as an aura source.

**Facet-1 verdict on the draft six:** biggest gap = the GAZE-RETURNED / DISTANCE-IN-CLOSENESS pair (Benjamin's actual core, not covered by the draft set — Facet 3 routes this through VECTOR's gaze-axis + PUNCTUM + the SOURCE/CONTRADICTION reads). Numinous overwhelm+draw is under-served (let SPATIAL PRESSURE carry the dwarf pole + VECTOR the draw). `register` is where person-reading leaks first → it became GRAIN/ERA. Unrepeatability gives the free aura SCALE.

## A2. Similar concepts cross-culturally (Facet 2)

**Headline:** Each tradition names a different SHAPE of charge — and the four most valuable read a WHOLE FIELD or a FLOW, exactly what the single-axis draft six miss.

| Concept | Tradition | The shape of charge | BLUE ROOM use |
|---------|-----------|---------------------|---------------|
| **Stimmung** | German Romantic (Goethe, Riegl, Spitzer 1944, Wellbery) | the TUNED WHOLE — one key all parts resolve to | → **TUNING** read (collision is its failure mode) |
| **Qi / 氣韻生動** | Chinese ink-painting (Xie He's First Law, c.500 CE) | DIRECTIONAL FLOW — a through-line that circulates or drains | → upgrade **VECTOR** to the whole circulatory path |
| **Atmosphäre / ecstasies** | Böhme's New Aesthetics 1993; Griffero | RADIATION OUTWARD — elements leak past their edges into negative space | distinct from spatial pressure (inward); pairs with Light/Color + Negative Space cues |
| **Vibe** | modern folk | FAST WHOLE-FIELD GESTALT (<1s) | → **VIBE-open** line |
| **Presence** | stage/photo; Benjamin's here-now | DOMINANCE OF MASS (bbox %, contrast, focal lock) | → **SOURCE** axis |
| **Mood** | Heidegger's Stimmung-as-Befindlichkeit; affect theory | SUSTAIN — held drone vs struck transient | → adds DECAY TIME to VECTOR's motion-state |
| **"It" factor / je-ne-sais-quoi** | casting / charisma | IRREDUCIBLE RESIDUE | → **IT-FACTOR residue-close** (confidence chip, not a lens) |
| **Ambiance** | Latin *ambire* | ENVELOPING ground-tone the setting supplies | → **SOURCE** axis (charge that survives swapping the subject) |

**Facet-2 verdict:** the draft six are single-axis; the real gap is whole-field. ADD Stimmung (tuning) + qi (flow upgrade to vector); collapse presence/ambiance/atmosphere into ONE SOURCE question ("place vs. gesture, radiate-out vs. claim-mass"); bookend with vibe-open + it-factor residue-close; add sustain to motion-state.

## A3. Is aura QUANTIFIABLE? (Facet 5) — the honest answer

**COMPUTE THE SURFACE, AUTHOR THE READ.** Aura straddles stimulus (the image, measurable) and response (the viewer's, not in the pixels). Only the stimulus half is computable.

**Russell's circumplex (1980)** splits affect into VALENCE (pleasant↔unpleasant) and AROUSAL (calm↔activated). The robust asymmetric finding: **AROUSAL** correlates with low-level image energy (contrast, saturation, brightness, edge density, sharpness) and is partially computable; **VALENCE** depends on content/meaning and is NOT reliably computable. This is the exact fault line aura sits on.

**COMPUTABLE — ship as measured cues with a confidence chip (these map ROBUSTLY to AROUSAL / intensity only):**
- low-key / key-to-fill ratio (luminance histogram shape + dark-pixel fraction) — the single most computable mood cue
- palette temperature (warm/cool pixel ratio, mean CIELAB a*/b*) — a number, but its "feeling" is the cliff
- **saturation + brightness** — the most replicated colour-emotion result (Valdez & Mehrabian 1994: these drive arousal far more reliably than hue drives valence)
- contrast · edge-density / clutter (Sobel/Canny) · sharpness/DoF
- dominant-line orientation (Hough) · symmetry · subject placement vs thirds

**UN-COMPUTABLE — must stay hand-authored / templated, never a number:**
- VALENCE (pleasant/unpleasant) · any named/specific emotion · the felt charge itself · all meaning.

**Why no emotion label:** Image-Emotion Recognition (Machajdik & Hanbury 2010; Zhao; FI / EmoSet) tops out ~55–70% on coarse categories, degrades hard out-of-distribution, and inter-annotator agreement is itself low (people don't agree what a photo "feels" like — the unclosed "affective gap"). A computed emotion label is confidently wrong AND a person-mood read → **double-banned.**

**Design implication:** build aura as a HYBRID — a small vector of measured surface descriptors (each with confidence) fed into a hand-authored/templated voice that converts surface-intensity into aura language, never crossing into valence, emotion-naming, or the person. **Rule: compute intensity, author meaning. Never a circumplex coordinate sold as truth.**

**Map to the lenses:** charge / spatial-pressure / contradiction = arousal-side (computable surface); temperature / grain-era / vector = valence-or-meaning-side (author-only, optics excepted).
