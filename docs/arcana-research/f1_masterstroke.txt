# THE MASTERSTROKE

**1. One line.** The room never explains a symbol; it files a draw — every deep page is an accession record of *this* reading, with the codex quoted as source.

**2. The governing idea.** The plan builds 154 pages "about Aries" and "about The Tower." Ten thousand sites already have those pages; that is what a well-built reference site is, and no polish escapes it. But this room's entire voice — "the mythic treated as filed record," "the marks are set," "Filed in the open" — is an archive's voice, and an archive holds no general knowledge. It holds *this* blade, accessioned *then*, from *this* dig. The moment a deep page speaks generically about a category, the fiction collapses and the museum becomes Wikipedia in a costume. The masterpiece move is to make the deep page about the draw as it fell: The Tower, mark IV of VIII, filed under The Ember-Banked Founder, contributor of "Founder" to the name borne — with the codex meaning rendered as a *quoted source document* inside the record, not as the page's own prose. The proof this is the missed node and not an invention: `arcana-name-engine.js` already returns a full `derivation` trail mapping every draw to the exact name-fragment it produced, plus per-reading `resonance` verdicts — a shipped, canonical, deterministic data product that the per-entry plan is structurally incapable of displaying, and which neither the master plan nor the adversarial audit mentions once.

**3. The single highest-leverage move.** Invert the identity of `entry.js`. Same file, same URL contract, same template, near-zero new data: when `from=seed` is present, the page's header IS the filing — crown name, chapter, "MARK IV OF VIII," and the line the derivation trail already computes ("This mark supplied *Founder* to the name borne") — and the meaning/`systemNote` sit below it on a visibly quoted SOURCE: CODEX plate. When no seed is present, the page opens with an explicit in-voice register: "UNFILED — codex source only. No record attached." Why it changes everything: it converts 154 commodity encyclopedia entries into pages that can only exist here; it makes the crown name a *verifiable sum* (click any draw, see the fragment it contributed — the reveal the name engine's own comments say the reader must be able to trace); it dissolves the audit's finding #9 correctly (draw and counsel on the same card are two accessions of one source, not a routing nuisance); and it deepens the ethics canon, because all depth binds to the artifact, never the person.

**4. What it makes us do differently.**
- **entry.js renders two registers, not one:** FILED (seed present: accession header, fragment line from `derivation`, resonance verdicts where the draw participates, quoted codex plate) and UNFILED (seedless: catalog copy, declared as such). This is the plan's Phase 4 with the hierarchy flipped — not a new phase.
- **Surface the derivation trail on the reading page finale:** the seal block gains the etymology the engine already returns — the name shown as its four parts, each attributed to its draws. The `EXPLORE →` link on each draw becomes "OPEN THE RECORD →" and carries seed+slot, because what opens is the record of the mark, not an article.
- **The `systemNote` and meaning are typeset as source, not voice:** inside the quoted plate they may keep encyclopedic register precisely because the page frames them as the codex being cited — the one place generic prose becomes canon-legal.
- **Sharing is solved by the register split, not by machinery:** copy the link with `from` and you share your filing; strip it and you share the catalog copy. No accounts, no save, no gallery — anti-paywall intact.
- **The marks brief inherits the frame:** the identity mark is an accession stamp (system emblem in archive grey, as the audit correctly reduced it), and the GPT brief is written as "stamp for a holding," not "logo for a concept."

**5. The one thing we must NOT do.** Do not build the 154 pages as freestanding encyclopedia articles first and plan to "add reading context later." The ornament version — generic page plus provenance stamp in the footer — is the audit's finding #9 frozen into shipped HTML; once the page's identity is the symbol, every later addition is a widget on a Wikipedia article, the derivation trail stays unread, and the room permanently speaks in two voices, one of which is everyone else's. The hierarchy is the masterpiece; it cannot be retrofitted.
