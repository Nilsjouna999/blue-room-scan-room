### 1. Extraction — what doc 03 actually proposes

**A. The two laws it proposes**

- **"Proposed law"** (loose): *"Name the depicted person, the image's charge, and the viewer's response in fully charged language, but never assert the subject's hidden interior state, fixed character, diagnosis, motive, or any reputation-bearing fact the frame cannot prove."* (doc 03, "Replacement law" section)
- **"House law (tighter)"**: *"Write the person only as the image gives them: visible action, pose, styling, context, and effect; never write their soul as fact."* (same section)

Doc 03 frames both as a replacement for what it calls an overread "blanket rule" ("never reference the person") — its stated target is the "artifact-not-person" principle, which it argues is "directionally right but easy to overread."

**B. The three-part boundary** ("Where criticism and ethics draw the boundary")

- **Safe core:** observable, image-tethered description of expression, posture, clothing, setting, cropping, light, gesture, and *the viewer's response*.
- **Conditionally safe:** interpretive wording clearly framed as the image's effect or reading, anchored to visible evidence.
- **Unsafe zone:** claims about hidden interior state, stable character, diagnosis, motive, criminality, or any reputation-bearing fact the image itself cannot prove.

**D. The move-the-adjective heuristic**

> "When a sentence starts to feel dangerous, move the adjective off the person and onto the **image, frame, gaze, encounter, pose, crop, light, or viewer**. The sentence usually gets stronger, not weaker."

Doc 03's generalized grammar move: replace **is** with **reads as / lands as / feels like / the image makes / the frame turns / the portrait stages / the viewer experiences.**

### 2. ADJUDICATION FRAME (mandatory — not softened)

**Repo law wins.** BR-S113 (docs/BR-AURA-RESEARCH.md, grep anchor `RULE 0 (governing boundary, LOCKED — BR-S113)`, line 5) is LOCKED and is stricter than doc 03's proposal:

> "A card reads the ARTIFACT, never the PERSON. Every aura read names an IMAGE-PROPERTY or a PERFORMED GESTURE-INTENT (the event in the frame), never the person's worth, body, rank, identity, or private feeling. If a sentence's grammatical subject is the person, it has fallen off the cliff."

**Doc 03's "Replacement law" (both the loose and the "tighter" house-law versions) is REJECTED as a replacement for BR-S113.** Reasons:

1. Doc 03's law explicitly licenses "the subject's hidden interior state... the image lands as loneliness/shame/etc." via a hinge clause. BR-S113's own kill-list (docs/BR-AURA-RESEARCH.md, grep anchor `### A. Aura cliché`, line 87) names exactly this construction — **"the resolved-feeling clause — the smuggling bridge: '…which evokes / conveys / creates a sense of …' Point at the fact and STOP"** — as banned. Doc 03's entire rewrite table launders named emotions through this exact bridge ("make the image land as loneliness," "give the portrait... the recoil of shame," "the sting of contempt," "sink the image into heaviness"). These are not surviving mechanics; they are the specific failure mode BR-S113 was written to kill.
2. Doc 03 treats "the viewer's response" as a license surface for felt-emotion labels ("the viewer feels," "the viewer experiences [emotion]"). BR-S113 does allow cue-bound viewer-transfer clauses, but only when the payload stays a spatial/optical fact, not a named emotion — repo's own safe example: **"the frame keeps you at arm's length — near but walled off by the turned shoulder and the glass"** (grep anchor `### B. Rule-0 safety-drift phrases`, line 94). That is "you stay at a distance," a geometry fact, not "you feel lonely."
3. Doc 03 makes **the depicted person the grammatical subject of the charge** in nearly every rewrite ("his presence hit the frame like a threat," "he reads as cornered," "she... make the picture run on attention" — the person is still doing the emotional/moral work, just with hedging language wrapped around it). BR-S113's test is exactly grammatical-subject-based and calls this "fallen off the cliff" regardless of hedging.
4. Doc 03's own worked examples still assign fixed character/reputation-bearing traits under a thin veneer — "a liar," "predatory," "calculating," "contemptuous," "insecure" all survive as the sentence's true payload, just relocated one clause to the right. BR-S113 bans exactly this category (worth/character, ranking, identity).

**Verdict: doc 03's proposed laws do not replace BR-S113. BR-S113 stands, unmodified, as the tighter and controlling law.**
