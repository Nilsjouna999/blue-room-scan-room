# BLUE ROOM — Halo Gate Boundary Lock v1

Layer: ACTIVE SPECS · Status: ACTIVE · Session BR-S030 (2026-06-13)

Locks the **honest boundary** between the complete Free Pull and the sealed
Halo back, *before* any Halo UI is built. Decision/spec only — **no runtime
change, no payment, no AI/backend/upload analysis, no Halo build.**

Builds on and **partially supersedes** `HALO_GATE_UPGRADE_LAYUP_V1` and
`FREE_PULL_SCREENSHOT_LAYOUT_V1` (see §H). Safety law inherits from
`PROJECT_OS` §1/§4, `CARD_LOGIC_V1` §1/§5, and the research human-in-frame /
language rules; nothing here relaxes it.

> **The core law:** Free Pull = the complete card **front**. Halo = the sealed
> card **back / dossier layer** of the *same* card. Free must never feel like
> "we hid your real result." Halo must never feel like "the real score is
> locked." Halo earns its price through **artifact depth**, never manipulation.

---

## A. Product frame

- **Free Pull is** the finished, screenshot-worthy card front: the developed
  image slab, artifact-safe stat **tier bands** (Muted/Clean/Strong/Charged/
  Peak), the archetype, 2 grounded receipt chips, the scope line, and a quiet
  **sealed-back hint** as atmosphere. The user already holds a complete artifact.
- **Halo is** the sealed **back** of that same card — a dossier layer of deeper
  *artifact production notes* (how the frame was built; what other frames would
  build). It is opened by intent, after the Free value has landed.
- **Halo is NOT:** a better card · a re-roll · a higher score · a gamble · a
  rarity chase · a personality/identity reveal · "your real result." It does
  not generate a new card; it opens the back of one you already own.
- **Why Halo exists after free value is delivered:** the Free Pull is a
  complete artifact, but a physical archive card *has* a back. Halo develops
  that back — extra depth on the same object — for people who want the full
  production record. It is additive depth, never the withheld "truth."

## B. Free Pull boundary (what the front MAY show)

The Free Pull front **may** show, and nothing heavier:

- the developed card front — image-first slab/card
- artifact-safe stat **tier bands** (no public 0–100; per `PROJECT_OS` §9)
- **2 short receipt chips** (`observed cue → artifact effect`; optional 3rd
  only if very short)
- **one** visible trigger / edition stamp **if obvious** (e.g. a Free Pull mark)
- a quiet **sealed-back hint** — *atmosphere only*

### The exact-count decision (LOCKED)

**The Free Pull front must NOT show exact locked counts.** No "2 image levers",
no "2 target variants", no "6 receipts found", no enumerated locked inventory.
Use **qualitative sealed-back language** instead.

**Why (this is the resolution of the earlier tension):** an exact count reads
as a *withheld inventory* — "we found 2 specific things and locked them from
you." That makes the Free Pull feel like an incomplete teaser ("we hid your
real result"), which violates the core law. Stating that a sealed back simply
**exists** keeps the card feeling complete while still hinting at depth.

**Honesty is preserved, not traded away.** The earlier specs justified counts
as honesty ("real counts — never inflated"). The honesty rule was always
*don't inflate / don't fake*. Qualitative existence ("a sealed back exists,
archived") is fully honest — the back genuinely exists; nothing is inflated or
invented. We drop *quantification*, not *truthfulness*. Exact counts and real
contents appear **only post-unlock**, inside the dossier the user chose to open.

| Allowed on the Free front (qualitative) | Banned on the Free front (exact / pressure) |
| --- | --- |
| Sealed back · archived | 2 image levers locked |
| This card has a sealed back. | 2 target variants hidden |
| Back face held in conservation. | Map exists — 2 image levers · 2 target variants |
| A dossier layer exists for this scan. | 6 receipts found |
| Additional production notes sealed. | Unlock full analysis |
| The back of this print is sealed. | See what we found about you |

## C. Halo Gate boundary (pre-unlock, before payment exists)

The Halo Gate is the sealed-back chamber reached by intent below/behind the
card. Before any unlock/payment exists it **may** show:

- the **sealed card back / archive back** metaphor (a closed plate, not a modal)
- a **non-specific dossier preview** — that a record is written and sealed
- on the dev route, explicit **"dev mock / not payment / not real analysis"** labels
- an **"open later" / "held in conservation"** mood
- **no** claim that a real analysis exists for an uploaded user photo

**Forbidden on the Halo Gate (any of these is a release blocker):**

- "the real score is hidden" / a hidden real result
- **exact locked result counts used as pressure** (same ban as §B)
- fake urgency · countdowns · streak pressure
- rarity / chance language · "almost rare" · "one away from mythic"
- "try again for better" · re-roll language
- "unlock your true self" · "we found something about you"
- subscription-trap language (recurring billing implied to see your result)
- psychological / personality / attractiveness / status / social-value claims

The gate proves there is something real behind it through the **metaphor and
the stated existence of a sealed record**, not through an enumerated locked
inventory.

## D. Halo Dossier boundary (define; do NOT build)

When Halo is eventually built, the opened (post-unlock) dossier **may** contain
these modules — each framed strictly as **artifact production notes / card-back
/ image levers**, never as advice about the person:

1. **The Read** — what the frame is doing.
2. **Strongest Lever** — the single image choice carrying the card.
3. **Three Active Levers** — the image levers in play (light, crop, distance, …).
4. **Keep This** — what already works in the frame.
5. **Two Variant Routes** — what a *different photo* would become (another card,
   not a "better" one).
6. **One Reduce** — one element the frame could drop.
7. **Next Pull Setup** — camera direction for the next shot.

**Framing law (LOCKED):** every module reads the **image / frame / artifact**.
None may read the person — no self-improvement, no diagnosis, no
attractiveness, personality, status, confidence, or social value. "Change the
photo, not yourself." A variant is *another card*, never a fixed/better you.
(See `docs/research/REFRAME_MAP_VALUE_SYSTEM_V1.md`.) Exact counts/contents are
fine **here** — this is the paid, opened back, not a pre-unlock teaser.

## F. Anti-patterns (bad copy + why it fails)

- **"Unlock your full score"** — implies a real result is hidden; Free Pull is
  complete, so this lies about the boundary.
- **"Map exists — 2 image levers · 2 target variants" / "2 hidden levers"** —
  exact locked-count pressure; reads as a withheld inventory, makes Free feel
  like a teaser (§B ban).
- **"6 receipts found · 3 locked"** — same withheld-inventory pressure.
- **"Almost rare" / "one away from mythic"** — chance / loot-box pressure.
- **"Find out what your photo says about you" / "we found something about you"**
  — person-truth / identity inference (fails the five-gate test).
- **"Improve your presence" / "unlock your true self"** — self-improvement /
  person rating, not artifact notes.
- **"Limited time" / a countdown** — fake urgency.
- **"Subscribe to see your result"** — subscription-trap; implies the result is
  held hostage behind recurring billing.
