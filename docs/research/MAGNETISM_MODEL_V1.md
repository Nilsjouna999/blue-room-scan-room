## The definition (the bar)
> **Canonical definition — see `GOLDEN_NUGGETS` #7** ("magnetism is pull by giving, not
> exploiting — provenance made legible, never scarcity/streaks"). We build only that. A surface earns return because the world is
> genuinely larger and more coherent — real structure made legible (a typed class, a numbered
> manifest, the latent→developed law), an honest artifact completed (the front is whole; the
> back is a different *mode*, not a withheld answer), or a place that feels like a SOMEWHERE
> (silence, light, materiality). The room remembers honestly (real local state, no
> "we-missed-you"), grows honestly (the advancing serial, the genuinely sealed back), and lives
> through **structure, not motion**.

**The operating test on every survivor:** it adds depth/comprehension/continuity/atmosphere
with **zero** countdown/scarcity/streak/anxiety; it is **feather-weight** (copy/CSS framing of
state already live in `data.js`/`app.js`, not new machinery); it **echoes** an existing
surface; and **it dies the instant its copy shifts from "this was partial / assigned on
Develop" to "unlock your score / you missed something better."**
