# BLUE ROOM — Parked Ideas (holding pen)

Far-beyond-core universe ideas — captured + lightly structured (2026-06-17, worked once with the
builder). Still not for action until the core (scan → mint card) is solid. Source files stay
authoritative; this is the idea workspace.

> Core first: the scan→mint card is the product. These are the rooms/surfaces the minted card
> *flows into* later — not separate products.

## The through-line (how to navigate all of these)

Every idea below is a **surface the minted card flows into**, tied together by the card's **serial**
(its address) and, where reactive, its **stats** (the input — that is the Kingdom mechanic exactly).
So the question for any of them is never "should this exist" in isolation — it is "what does *the
card* do when it arrives here." This is the "card is the crown / link" heading playing out.

## The guard (carry over all of them)

Anything touching Friends / Vault / Socials / a referral person-edge is where a **person-join**
tempts — and that is the LOCKED aggregate-refusal wall: **object-keyed (scan serial → filing-event →
mint #), never person-keyed** (inherits `UNIVERSE_ZONE_MAP_V1` §7; re-derive fresh per scan, no
streaks/counts/history). The future *chance-rarity on develop* idea sits under the same family of
guards — unreconciled against the magnetism kill-rule, research-later.

## The ideas (precise locus, not a build list)

- **Idle Room → Kingdom** — the *room* is confirmed; the *fill* is open. **Kingdom** is the leading
  fill: a card is placed → the card's **stats drive** an *idle* (self-advancing) reactive place;
  *what* it evokes (state / growth / events) is open. Tempo = idle (advances on its own).
- **Profile + Vault** — two identity surfaces; the **interconnection between them is the design
  object** (more than either alone). Vault contents are *deliberately* unknown — a thing to decide,
  held open on purpose.
- **Codex vs Blogs/Journals** — two registers of writing doing different jobs: **Codex** = structured,
  canonical, in-universe reference (the home for the numerology / zodiac / MBTI *lore*, fed by the
  decided **Profile Layers** mechanism — `TASK_QUEUE` Backlog; user-declared, never inferred; canon
  `GOLDEN_NUGGETS` #9); **Blogs/Journals** = looser, ongoing, about/within the universe. The *split*
  is the idea, deliberately not one thing.
- **Friends** — a social graph on identity; that it *exists* is named, what it *does* (see your
  vault? touch your kingdom? just connect?) is undetermined. (Governed by the guard above.)
- **Card autobattler** — a *play-layer slot* in the universe is committed; its *form* is NOT
  (combat / interactive / minimal / minigames = the *range* of fills, not four ideas). Lowest
  conviction, stated as such.
- **Socials (interlinking)** — **deferred-conditional**: built only *if* we later find a real way to
  spin them (non-redundant, doing a job nothing else does). Not "just because" — the condition *is*
  the idea (pre-refusing bolted-on socials).

## Pulled OUT of the pen (decided / core-adjacent — not parked)

- **One paid reading** — a *singular, bounded* paid action (free → **one** paid card reading, not a
  tier or open spend). Matches the decided paid-read spine → it lives **core-side** (`TASK_QUEUE`
  Backlog), not here.
- **Referral → earns a paid reading** ("or similar") — the builder's idea: a successful referral
  *grants* the paid reading. Pure **magnetism-by-giving** (earn the deep read by growing the archive,
  never scarcity). Build-side growth mechanic, kept **bounded** (one clean referral → one reading; no
  streaks/leaderboards; object-keyed). In `TASK_QUEUE` Backlog.
